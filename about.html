About Us

We are fully insured and have been approved by NAPIT and listed on the Electrical Safety Register. We primarily serve the North London and Hertfordshire area but also offer our services to surrounding locations. We have a track record of providing high-quality electrical services to both residential and commercial clients.

Our range of electrical services is frequently utilized by building contractors, as well as homeowners and business owners. We offer periodic testing and inspections, LED lighting installation, kitchen electrical work, CCTV camera installation, and audio equipment setup. Let us provide a professional service tailored to your needs.
