Why choose us?

We are a team of skilled electricians located in Hertfordshire. We are listed on the Electrical Safety Register and are fully insured and NAPIT approved contractors. Our services are mainly available in North London and Hertfordshire, but we are willing to travel to nearby areas as well..

Our team is equipped to handle all aspects of your electrical system, including installation, extension, and adaptation of all circuits. Ranging from consumer unit upgrades to new build homes. We also specialize in the installation and replacement of light fixtures and switches, and have the expertise to design and install new lighting systems.

Our team is fully qualified to perform test inspections, diagnosis, and fault finding on electrical systems. As professional electricians, we are committed to complying with the latest regulations and requirements to ensure that all work is completed to the highest standard.

We offer Electrical Installation Condition Reports, PAT Testing, and Annual Emergency Lightning & Fire Alarm Reports. Landlords are legally required to have their properties' electrical installations inspected and tested by qualified and competent professionals. Our team is equipped to handle these services for you.
