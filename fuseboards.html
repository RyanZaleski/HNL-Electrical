Fuse Boards

Fuse boxes control the distribution and flow of electricity around your property. As local electrical contractors, we are qualified to undertake fuse box inspections in accordance with UK Electrical Regulations. Fixing a fuse box

We often overlook fuse boxes until the electricity suddenly goes out. Tripping occurs when the fuse box tries to control a surge in electricity. It is frustrating, but it is intended. It is essential to keep you safe.

Typically, a one-off occurrence is not a cause for concern. Checking the appliances plugged in and switching the mains switch may be sufficient to resolve the issue. Continued problems may indicate a faulty appliance or a fuse box issue. You should not attempt to repair the fuse box yourself. This is a job for a qualified Electrician.

We, as electrical contractors, test, diagnose, and resolve problems, when possible. If your fuse box does not comply with regulations or cannot be repaired, it will need to be replaced.

Replacing a fuse box

During renovations or extensions, our electricians are also called upon to replace a fuse box. The current fuse box may not be sufficient if you plan on adding more electrical outlets, switches, and appliances. Upgrading your fuse box will ensure that you have the power required, when and where you need it. We provide you with a NICEIC Electrical Installation certificate for your records and Part B notification to Building Control as part of our fuse box replacemnet electrical services. All our electricians work to the highest safety standards and you can trust that all our work will be fully compliant with UK Electrical Regulations.
